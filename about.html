Our Story

Fine dining with a human touch

Founded in 2025, Suvaitham blends seasonal ingredients with refined technique. Our chefs craft menus that are creative yet approachable. Suvaitham was born from a simple idea — to bring the true flavors of Tamil Nadu to every plate with honesty, warmth, and care. From the spice of Chettinad curries to the comfort of soft idiyappam, every dish carries the essence of our homeland. Whether you're a local or a traveler, experience the soul of Tamil cuisine — cooked with passion and served with pride.
